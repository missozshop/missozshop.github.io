About

Message from Alexandra Ozmeral

People ask me where I get my outfits, shoes, and creams every single day, so I created this website to show off some of my favorite looks of my last semester at USC. However, picking cute outfits is not all it takes to adopt my style. One must learn to be confident, positive, and live in the moment. And finally, a special thanks to my greatest inspiration and the words she taught me to live by:

"Beauty, to me, is about being comfortable in your own skin. That, or a kick-ass red lipstick."

Gwyneth Paltrow
